Voice4VOICE

For many years, LGBT+ members struggle with the idea of expressing themselves. They fear that by sharing how they feel and/or coming out, they will dismantle their existing relationships with family and/or friend.

As a member of the LGBT+ community, I can say first-hand that the journey in itself is overwhelming. There were so many things that I was trying to figure out all at once about myself. I was too scared to confide in anyone and wished that there was a place for me to feel open and free to express with no worry.

Voice4VOICE is a social outlet that helps individuals within the LGBT+ community tackle their inner struggles by enabling them to display their truths in hopes that others will be inspired to display theirs. Hence the title, “Voice4VOICE”.

As an entry for Voice4VOICE, my team and I decided to construct an interview where we asked an individual to take part in a Q&A in relation to their personal experiences and how they overcame certain obstacles throughout their life.

INTERVIEW 1: Christina Paris

This is Christina Paris, a 19 year old young female who identifies herself as Hetero-Romantic Bisexual. In this interview, she discussed in depth her reasoning behind this label as well as how her personal experiences led her to her self discovery.
